About the Organizers

Daniel

Daniel Fradin is a senior from Studio City, California studying English and Cinema Studies. He has been getting more into soup as a concept. The soup is interesting; it can be both a meal and a preparatory dish. Further, one never knows whether they're 'eating' soup or 'drinking' soup, complicating the issue of the soup. IHHO (In His Humble Opinion), one drinks soup, any soup that can be considered an 'eating'soup is not a soup at all, but a stew or a goulash.

Alex

Alex Fisher is a senior studying History of Art and Economics from Buffalo, New York. To label his skills in the kitchen 'novice' would be far too generous, but he loves making a space where those more talented can shine. He collects golf balls (3,000+ to date), stickers, magnets, and hockey jerseys from Eastern Europe. When not coordinating Dinner Project, he runs a postcard residency on his Frigidaire. His go-to campus meal is a Hemo's sausage, egg, spinach and cheese with extra hot sauce.

Naomi

Naomi Pohl is a Masters student in Computer and Information Technology from Morristown, New Jersey. She studied Math at Penn undergrad and graduated in May 2018. In her free time, she can be found perfecting her mushroom risotto recipe, laughing until she cries over a David Sedaris book, or scouring Google Flights for cheap weekend trips. She also enjoys teaching girls how to code and creating inclusive spaces on campus. Her favorite Philly meal is Square Pie, which she easily eats once a month ¯\_(ツ)_/¯
